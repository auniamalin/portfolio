Dr. Aquascape

An Integrated E-Commerce Ordering System

Final Year Project | Individual Project | Collaboration with Dr. Aquascape Enterprise

Project Overview

Aquascaping has become increasingly popular as a way to bring natural aesthetics into homes and workspaces. However, customers often face difficulties finding reliable suppliers, comparing products, exploring aquascape designs, and understanding the costs involved. At the same time, many aquascaping businesses still rely on traditional sales processes - making it challenging to showcase their products and efficiently manage customer orders.

To address these challenges, I collaborated with Dr. Aquascape Enterprise to develop an integrated e-commerce ordering system as my Final Year Project. The system provides customers with a centralized platform to browse aquascaping products, explore design inspirations, and place orders online, while enabling administrators to manage products, customer information, and orders more efficiently through a dedicated management portal.

This project focused on improving the customer purchasing experience, streamlining business operations, and supporting the digital transformation of a local aquascaping business through a modern web-based solution.

System Features

Customer Portal

  • Access About Us page to learn about the company background and services.
  • View past aquascaping projects for design inspiration.
  • Contact the business through an integrated contact form.
  • Register and log in to access personalized features.
  • Manage and update user profile information.
  • Browse aquascaping products with categories and filtering options.
  • View detailed product information including specifications and pricing.
  • Manage selected items through a shopping cart system.
  • Provide delivery information during the checkout process.
  • Complete secure online payments for orders.
  • Track order history and previous purchases.
  • Submit product ratings and reviews based on user experience.

Administrator Portal

  • Monitor overall business performance through an admin dashboard.
  • Manage customer records and account information.
  • Process and update order statuses efficiently.
  • Perform full CRUD operations for product management.
  • Handle and track payment records and transactions.
  • Manage customer ratings and product reviews for quality control.

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge 1

Designing an ordering workflow that was simple for customers while meeting business requirements.

Solution

Created an intuitive shopping flow with organized product categories, shopping cart functionality, and streamlined checkout to improve the purchasing experience.

Challenge 2

Managing multiple business operations in a single platform.

Solution

Separated customer-facing features from the administrator dashboard using role-based authentication, making system management more organized and secure.

Challenge 3

Developing the project independently within the project timeline.

Solution

Applied Agile development by breaking the project into smaller milestones, allowing continuous testing and improvements throughout development.
